The 2026 Bafana Bafana Home Jersey is an official Adidas × SAFA collaboration celebrating South African football heritage. The striking yellow colourway with vibrant green Adidas three-stripe detailing and embroidered SAFA crest captures the pride and passion of the nation. Crafted from premium recycled polyester with Adidas's AEROREADY moisture-wicking technology, it keeps you cool and dry whether you're watching from the stands or playing on the pitch.
