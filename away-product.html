The 2026 Bafana Bafana Away Jersey is an official Adidas × SAFA collaboration that embodies the fierce spirit of South African football on the road. The bold forest-green colourway with authentic gold Adidas three-stripe detailing and embroidered SAFA crest pays tribute to decades of South African football passion.

Crafted from premium recycled polyester with Adidas's AEROREADY moisture-wicking technology, it keeps you cool and dry whether you're watching from the stands or playing on the pitch.
